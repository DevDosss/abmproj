Composition

Composition is the arrangement of elements in a work of art, like a photograph. It's important for creating dynamic images and telling a story.

Simplification

Simplification in photography means removing clutter to focus on the main subject.




Photography Techniques

Rule of Thirds

Divide the frame into three equal sections and position your subject along the lines or intersections. This creates natural balance and draws the viewer's attention.

Shutter Speed

How long the camera's shutter stays open. A slow shutter speed allows more light but can cause motion blur, and a fast shutter speed freezes motion but lets in less light.

ISO

Measures the camera sensor's sensitivity to light. A high ISO brightens the image but can induce noise (grain), while a low ISO gives a sharp, clean image but requires more light.
